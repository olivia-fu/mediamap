Welcome to our Media Tourism Map!

We are Yejin and Olivia, and this is our joint research project between CS50 and East Asian Media Studies at Harvard. We sought to understand the connection between media representation in anime films and tourism to featured locations in Japan, and explored data visualization techniques to map out our findings.

Our maps include tourism numbers to each prefecture by year, and highlight locations of popular anime films.
